PRIVACY POLICY

Effective: March 2025  ·  Last updated: March 2025
// 01
WHO WE ARE

String Theory is a product by Dystopic Labs. We built this tool to help you map the pivotal events of your life onto a visual timeline and explore how your path might have unfolded differently.

Contact: Contact form →

// 02
WHAT WE COLLECT

We only collect what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Email address — only if you choose to save your timeline. Used solely to retrieve your data on future visits.
  • Timeline data — your quiz answers, life events, AI-enriched descriptions, and visualisation settings. Stored as a single JSON record linked to your email.

We do not collect names, payment information, device identifiers, or behavioural/tracking data. We do not use cookies for advertising.

// 03
HOW WE USE IT
  • To save and restore your timeline when you return.
  • To send you a return link if you request one.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not use your data to train AI models.
// 04
AI PROCESSING

When you complete the quiz, your answers are sent to an AI model (via Groq) to enrich your timeline descriptions. This processing happens in real time and is not retained by the AI provider beyond the immediate API call, per their data processing terms.

The AI provider is Groq, Inc. — see their privacy policy at groq.com/privacy-policy.

// 05
DATA STORAGE

Your timeline data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted by Neon (neon.tech), which operates on AWS infrastructure in the US. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

// 06
YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your timeline and email from our database at any time.
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent — stop us from holding your data at any time with no penalty.

To exercise any of these rights, use our contact form and select the relevant request type. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are in the EU/EEA, these rights are provided under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you are in California, these rights are provided under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

// 07
CHILDREN

String Theory is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has submitted data to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

// 08

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the service after a change constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
